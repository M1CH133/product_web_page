About

Reality Check is a website that sells VR accessories to help you create your own reality. Virtual Reality (VR) is a computer-generated simulation of a three-dimensional environment that can be interacted with in a seemingly real or physical way. With VR technology, you can put on a headset that consists of a screen and lenses in front of your eyes, headphones, and sometimes handheld controllers, and enter a virtual environment that you can experience as if you were physically present. Reality Check offers a wide range of VR accessories to enhance your VR experience, such as high-quality headsets, handheld controllers, motion sensors, and more. These accessories can help you fully immerse yourself in the virtual world and create your own reality by interacting with it in a more realistic and intuitive way. Whether you're looking for the latest VR technology or accessories to improve your existing setup, Reality Check has something for everyone.
